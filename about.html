About Adveris

Where Expertise Meets Integrity.

Our Story

Founded on Purpose

Adveris Advisors LLP was founded in Bengaluru with a clear and singular purpose: to democratise access to high-quality legal and compliance services for businesses across India.

We saw a gap in the market — quality advisory was often reserved for large corporations with deep pockets. We built Adveris to change that, bringing enterprise-grade legal expertise to startups, SMEs, and growing companies at every stage of their journey.

Today, we serve clients across sectors including technology, manufacturing, financial services, real estate, and e-commerce — all under one roof, with a team that truly understands the Indian regulatory landscape.

Our Foundation

Core Values

The principles that guide every engagement, every decision, and every relationship we build.

Integrity First

We believe honesty is non-negotiable. Our advice is always unbiased, fully transparent, and in the best interest of our clients — even when it's difficult to hear.

Deep Expertise

Our team holds professional certifications from ICSI, Bar Council and other recognised bodies. We invest continuously in staying current with India's evolving regulatory framework.

We leverage technology to deliver faster, more accurate, and more cost-effective compliance solutions — without ever compromising on the quality of advice.
