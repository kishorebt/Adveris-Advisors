What We Do

Our Practice Areas.

Comprehensive Legal Services

Eight Areas of Deep Expertise

From company formation and board governance to regulatory filings and transaction advisory — we are your end-to-end legal partner across India.

01

Corporate Law

Comprehensive corporate legal advisory covering company formation, restructuring, mergers, boardroom governance, and statutory compliance. We handle the full lifecycle of your corporate entity — from incorporation to winding-up — in accordance with the Companies Act, 2013.

Our corporate law practice advises directors, shareholders and promoters on their fiduciary duties, NCLT proceedings, and corporate secretarial governance under MCA norms.

Companies Act 2013 · NCLT Rules · SEBI Regulations
02

Mergers & Acquisitions

End-to-end M&A advisory from initial due diligence to deal closure. We advise acquirers, targets and investors on structuring, valuation, regulatory approvals and post-merger integration in compliance with Indian law.

Our team handles cross-border transactions, FEMA compliance, CCI approvals, scheme filings with NCLT, and all attendant corporate law aspects under the Companies Act and SEBI Takeover Code.

Takeover Code · FEMA · Companies Act 2013 · CCI Act
03

Regulatory Compliance

Proactive, calendar-driven compliance management with key regulators including SEBI, RBI, and MCA. We help listed companies, NBFCs, FPIs, and foreign entities stay compliant with India's dense regulatory framework.

Services include LODR compliance, FEMA/ODI/FDI reporting, PMLA compliance, RBI registrations, and MCA e-form filings. We provide monthly compliance calendars and ongoing monitoring alerts.

SEBI LODR · FEMA · RBI Circulars · MCA21 · PMLA
04

Company Secretary Services

Professional CS services delivered by ICSI-qualified Company Secretaries. We manage all ROC filings, annual returns, board meeting minutes, AGM and EGM documentation, and maintenance of statutory registers.

With the new MCA21 V3 portal, we ensure error-free, timely e-form submissions including AOC-4, MGT-7, DIR-3 KYC, and all other annual and event-based filings to avoid penalties.

Companies Act 2013 · MCA21 V3 · ICSI Guidelines · ROC Rules
05

Contract Drafting & Review

Expertly drafted and reviewed commercial contracts protecting your business interests at every stage. From NDAs and vendor agreements to complex SaaS contracts, shareholder agreements and joint venture instruments.

Our drafting combines Indian Contract Act principles with sector-specific requirements, including IT Act provisions for digital contracts, RERA clauses for real estate, and RBI guidelines for NBFC agreements.

Indian Contract Act · IT Act · RERA · Specific Relief Act
06

Labour & Employment Law

Full-spectrum employment law advisory — from drafting employment contracts, HR policies, and POSH frameworks to representation in labour disputes and Industrial Tribunal matters.

We advise on compliance with the new Labour Codes (Wages, Industrial Relations, Social Security, and Occupational Safety) and support businesses in transitioning their HR practices to meet the new regulatory requirements.

Labour Codes 2020 · POSH Act · Industrial Disputes Act · EPF & ESIC
07

GST & Tax Compliance

End-to-end GST compliance — registration, return filing, reconciliation, audit support, and advisory on complex classification and ITC matters. We also handle GST litigation and representation before tax authorities.

Our tax practice covers advance tax planning, TDS compliance, income tax return filing for corporates and LLPs, and advisory on domestic and international tax structuring aligned with DTAA provisions.

CGST Act · IGST Act · Income Tax Act · TDS Rules · DTAA
08

Startup Legal Advisory

Dedicated legal support for startups at every stage — from choosing the right entity structure (LLP vs Pvt Ltd), DPIIT Startup recognition, to fundraising documentation (term sheets, SHA, SSA) and ESOP policy drafting.

We guide founders through investor due diligence, Series A/B documentation, regulatory sandbox applications, and compliance frameworks that scale with their growth — built for lean teams with zero legal overhead.

DPIIT Policy · SEBI AIF Regulations · FEMA · Companies Act 2013
