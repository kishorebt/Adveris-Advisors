FAQs

Common Questions

How quickly can you onboard my company?

Most engagements begin within 2–3 business days of signing the engagement letter. Urgent compliance matters can be addressed within 24 hours.

Do you serve clients outside Bengaluru?

Yes — we operate pan-India. Most services are delivered remotely via our secure document portal. Physical meetings can be arranged for Bengaluru clients.

What is your pricing model?

We offer both monthly retainer packages (for ongoing compliance) and fixed-fee project engagements (for one-time mandates). All pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.

Do you offer a free initial consultation?

Yes — we offer a complimentary 30-minute discovery call to understand your needs and explain how we can help. No commitment required.
